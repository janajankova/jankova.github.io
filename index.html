I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Statistical Laboratory advised by Professor Richard Samworth. Prior to joining the University of Cambridge, I was a PhD student at the Seminar for Statistics, ETH Zurich where I was fortunate to work with Professor Sara van de Geer. I obtained my BSc and MSc Degrees from the Free University in Amsterdam and Comenius University in Bratislava.

Research

My research interests are primarily in the area of high-dimensional data analysis, statistical inference and machine learning. My papers can be found at Google Scholar.
Some of my current research interests include:
  • High-dimensional statistics, Lasso
  • Graphical models
  • Covariance estimation
  • Eigenstructure estimation
  • Information theory, lower bounds
  • Machine learning
